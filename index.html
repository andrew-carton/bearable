A trophy secured, a heart maintained


Burned across the heart remain
My burned mission, in heartless gain
Blight upon a poor man's glee
A safe man found, secured to a tea


And then by night, my fallen glee
A lamp in the desert, a brave coffee
Bound to leak, the bar hung tight
My failed mission, secured through flight


A child slain, through witchcraft might
The dagger fallen in one hand tight
Born through sorrow, my mission maintain
A fallen comfort, my lips silent remain
